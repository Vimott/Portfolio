Web Design

Welcome to this simple introduction to web design using HTML and colors.

HTML Structure

HTML forms the backbone of every web page. It lets you create:

  • Headings to organize content clearly
  • Paragraphs to present information
  • Lists for grouping related items
  • Links to connect different pages and sites
  • Images to add visual interest

Adding Color

Colors help make your web page more engaging and easier to read. Using inline styles, you can change the color of any text or background.

For example, this paragraph has a violet background with white text to highlight important information.

Why Colors Matter

Choosing the right colors improves user experience, enhances readability, and helps communicate the right mood or message. The rainbow colors here show how you can make your design lively and inviting.
